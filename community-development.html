Our Programs

Community Development

We provide holistic community development initiatives that empower communities to identify their priorities, take ownership of local solutions, and lead sustainable change. Our approach ensures that development is community-driven, participatory, and accountable, engaging local leaders, youth, women, and marginalized groups. Our initiatives guide communities in developing Community Action Plans (CAPs)—structured strategies that outline local development priorities, resource needs, and actionable steps. These CAPs are shared with donors, government agencies, NGOs, and other development partners, ensuring coordinated support, funding, and policy alignment. Through capacity-building, participatory planning, and facilitation of community governance structures, we promote inclusive leadership, social cohesion, and self-reliance. Our work spans health, education, livelihoods, infrastructure, environmental stewardship, and peacebuilding—integrating these into locally-owned strategies that drive long-term change.

What We Do

Core Activities

Community Needs Assessment

Participatory diagnostics to identify priorities, strengths, and gaps.

Community Action Plan (CAP) Development

Facilitating participatory planning sessions to produce CAPs with clear goals, activities, indicators, and timelines.

Capacity-Building for Community Leadership

Training local leaders, committees, and youth groups to implement CAPs and mobilize resources.

Inclusive Governance Structures

Establishing committees and forums that promote accountability, transparency, and participation from all community groups.

Linkage to Donors & Agencies

Sharing CAPs with donors, government agencies, and NGOs to attract support and funding for community-led projects.

Monitoring & Support

Guiding communities through implementation of CAPs, documenting outcomes, and adapting strategies for maximum impact.

Cross-Sector Integration

Integrating health, education, livelihoods, environmental action, and peacebuilding into community-led plans.
