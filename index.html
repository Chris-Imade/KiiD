About US

Creating a Society Where Everyone Thrives

Kingscity Initiative for Integrated Development is a registered nonprofit organization dedicated to building thriving communities by providing equitable access to healthcare, fostering climate awareness, and promoting socio-economic empowerment. We work with underserved populations including women, youth, vulnerable individuals, and vulnerable groups.

Our Vision

  • A society where every individual, regardless of background, enjoys dignity, health, opportunity, and a sustainable environment.

Meet Our Team

Dedicated Professionals

Our multidisciplinary team is composed of seasoned social workers, development professionals, renewable energy and climate change specialists, gender equality experts, civil society leaders, and grant management professionals. With decades of collective experience across humanitarian and development settings, we are well-equipped to design and implement inclusive, sustainable, and results-driven interventions. Our team's diverse expertise ensures a holistic approach that prioritizes accountability, community engagement, and alignment with donor goals—particularly in advancing gender equity, climate resilience, and social protection for vulnerable populations.
