Who We Are

Building Healthy, Resilient & Empowered Communities

Kingscity Initiative for Integrated Development (KIID) is a community-driven organization committed to building healthy, resilient, and empowered communities in the society.

We deliver integrated development solutions in health, environmental sustainability, socio-economic empowerment, protection, MHPSS, education, capacity strengthening, food security, peace and conflict resolution, cross-cutting community development and evidence-driven research and evaluation.

Our Focus Areas

What We Do

We focus on three core pillars of development that create integrated solutions for communities:

1. Health Advocacy and Free Healthcare Services

We reach underserved communities with mobile medical services, maternal and child health support, and disease prevention education. Our partnerships with healthcare professionals, funders and NGOs ensure the underserved have access to quality care.

2. Environmental Protection and Climate Change Awareness

We promote eco-literacy through climate clubs, tree planting campaigns, WASH programs and renewable energy workshops. Our environmental advocacy focuses on waste management, urban greening, and sustainable living.

3. Socio-Economic Empowerment for Women, Youth, and Vulnerable Groups

Our initiatives include vocational training, entrepreneurship development, microfinance access, cooperative societies, and agribusiness support to women, youth, and vulnerable persons. Reintegration programs also provide mental health and psychosocial support, child protection, disability inclusion, family strengthening and protection services for GBV, displaced and vulnerable individuals.
