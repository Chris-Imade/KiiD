About US

Kingscity Initiative for Integrated Development

Kingscity Initiative for Integrated Development is a registered nonprofit organization dedicated to building thriving communities by providing equitable access to healthcare, fostering climate awareness, and promoting socio-economic empowerment. We work with underserved populations including women, youth, vulnerable individuals, and marginalized groups, using holistic development programs to improve lives sustainably.

Our Mission

  • To implement integrated development programs that improve healthcare access, promote environmental sustainability, and empower vulnerable populations through education, enterprise, and reintegration support.

Mission, Vision, and Core Values

Get Involved

Our Mission

To implement integrated development programs that improve healthcare access, promote environmental sustainability, and empower vulnerable populations through education, enterprise, and reintegration support.

  • Providing equitable healthcare access to underserved communities
  • Promoting environmental awareness and sustainability
  • Supporting socio-economic empowerment for vulnerable groups
  • Facilitating reintegration for vulnerable individuals

Our programs span community health outreach, maternal and child health services, climate clubs, waste recycling education, skills acquisition, community development, and microfinance support to create holistic development impact.

Our Vision

To create a society where every individual, regardless of background, enjoys dignity, health, opportunity, and a sustainable environment.

  • Equal access to quality healthcare for all
  • Environmental awareness and protection in communities
  • Economic opportunities for vulnerable populations
  • Dignified reintegration for displaced individuals
  • Community-led sustainable development
  • Resilient families and strengthened social support systems

Our Core Values

Our work is guided by seven fundamental values that shape everything we do:

  • Inclusiveness: Everyone deserves access to opportunities.
  • Integrity: We maintain transparency and accountability.
  • Compassion: We serve with empathy and human dignity.
  • Sustainability: We prioritize lasting, community-owned impact.
  • Empowerment: We enable individuals to become self-reliant.
  • Innovation: We embrace technology and creative solutions.
  • Collaboration: We believe in partnerships for shared progress.
Our Focus Areas

What We Do

We focus on three core pillars of development that create integrated solutions for communities:

1. Health Advocacy and Free Healthcare Services

We reach underserved communities with mobile medical services, maternal and child health support, and disease prevention education. Our partnerships with healthcare professionals, funders and NGOs ensure the underserved have access to quality care.

2. Environmental Protection and Climate Change Awareness

We promote eco-literacy through climate clubs, tree planting campaigns, WASH programs and renewable energy workshops. Our environmental advocacy focuses on waste management, urban greening, and sustainable living.

3. Socio-Economic Empowerment for Women, Youth, and Vulnerable Groups

Our initiatives include vocational training, entrepreneurship development, microfinance access, cooperative societies, and agribusiness support to women, youth, and vulnerable persons. Reintegration programs also provide mental health and psychosocial support, child protection, disability inclusion, family strengthening and protection services for GBV, displaced and vulnerable individuals.
