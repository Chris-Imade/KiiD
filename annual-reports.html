Our Annual Reports & Financial Statements

Explore our annual reports to see how your support is making a difference in communities across Nigeria. Our reports provide detailed information about our programs, impact, and financial stewardship.

2023 Annual Report

Our 2023 annual report highlights the significant strides made in healthcare outreach, climate action, and socio-economic empowerment programs across our communities.

  • Health programs reached 1,000+ people
  • Environmental initiatives planted 5,000+ trees
  • Skills training for 150+ individuals

2022 Annual Report

In 2022, we expanded our community reach and launched new initiatives focused on maternal health and youth climate action programs.

  • Health outreach expanded to 2 new communities
  • Climate clubs established in 10 schools
  • Reintegration support for 20+ returnees

2021 Annual Report

Our foundation year focused on establishing our core programs and building community relationships in Edo State, Abuja, and Enugu State.

  • Initial health program established
  • First tree planting initiative launched
  • Skills training pilot program completed
