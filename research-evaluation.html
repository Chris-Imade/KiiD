Our Programs

Research & Evaluation

We deliver evidence-based research that informs decision-making, strengthens accountability, and drives program improvement.

What We Do

Core Activities

Needs Assessments

Comprehensive community and organizational assessments to identify gaps and inform program design.

Program Monitoring & Evaluation

Tracking program progress, measuring outcomes, and ensuring accountability through data-driven insights.

Impact Studies

Rigorous evaluation of program effectiveness and long-term community impact using qualitative and quantitative methods.

Data Collection & Analysis

Systematic gathering and analysis of data to support evidence-based decision making and policy advocacy.

Research Publications

Documenting findings and sharing knowledge through reports, case studies, and academic publications.

Real Change

Impact Stories

"KiiD's baseline assessment revealed critical gaps in WASH infrastructure across 15 communities, leading to a $200,000 donor-funded intervention that now serves 5,000 people."

"Our M&E framework helped a partner NGO demonstrate 85% improvement in program outcomes, securing continued funding for three more years."

"Research findings from our youth empowerment study informed state policy changes, benefiting over 10,000 young people across Nigeria."
