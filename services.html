Our Work

Our Programs

KIID implements integrated, people-centered development programs designed to tackle the most pressing challenges facing communities today—from healthcare gaps and climate vulnerabilities to unemployment, weak educational systems, food insecurity, peacebuilding needs, and protection and mental health concerns. Through compassionate, evidence-driven, and community-led approaches, we work to build healthier, safer, and more resilient communities.

Health Advocacy & Outreach

We provide life-saving and preventive health services that bring care directly to underserved communities. We reach out through mobile medical outreach programs that ensure families in hard-to-reach locations receive timely healthcare and essential screenings. Our initiatives promote maternal and child health, strengthen disease prevention practices, and enhance community knowledge through hygiene promotion and health education campaigns.

Learn More

Environmental Protection & Climate Action

We promote climate-smart community actions that help people adapt to and reduce the impacts of climate change. We provide hands-on environmental stewardship programs, including tree-planting, afforestation drives, and school climate clubs—that inspire young environmental leaders. Our initiatives support clean energy adoption, advance waste reduction and recycling, and expand access to clean water and sanitation through robust WASH interventions.

Learn More

Socio-Economic Empowerment

We empower individuals, especially youth and women, with practical skills and opportunities to build sustainable livelihoods. We provide vocational and technical training, entrepreneurship support, and micro-grants that help start and grow small businesses. Our initiatives promote financial literacy, strengthen income-generating capacities, and create career development pathways that prepare young people for stable and meaningful employment.

Learn More

Protection & Mental Health and Psychosocial Support (MHPSS)

We provide holistic protection services combined with trauma-informed Mental Health and Psychosocial Support (MHPSS) to safeguard dignity and strengthen emotional resilience. We reach out with counselling, psychological first aid, safe spaces, disability-inclusive services, and reintegration support for survivors of violence, exploitation, or crisis. Our initiatives promote community awareness on trafficking, Gender-Based Violence (GBV), mental health, and protection risks while strengthening local support systems.

Learn More

Capacity Building & Consulting

We provide high-quality training, advisory services, and institutional strengthening to organizations and community structures. Our initiatives build leadership capacities, improve governance systems, and enhance local institutions through targeted workshops and organizational assessments. We support partners with proposal development, project design, reporting, and strategic consulting that enhances program quality and long-term impact.

What We Offer:
  • Training & Workshops (Health, WASH, Climate Action, Leadership, GBV & Protection, MEAL)
  • Organizational Development & Governance Strengthening
  • Technical Consulting (Project Design, M&E Systems, Quality Assurance)
  • Coaching & Mentorship Programs

Research & Evaluation

We promote evidence-driven development through rigorous research, assessments, and evaluations. We provide comprehensive M&E support, including baseline, midline, and endline studies; community needs assessments; and impact evaluations that inform policy and programming. Our initiatives produce high-quality data analysis, reports, and research publications that strengthen decision-making and advance development practice.

Our Services:
  • Baseline, Midline & Endline Evaluations
  • Needs Assessments & Impact Studies
  • M&E System Strengthening
  • Data Collection & Analytics
  • Publications & Policy Briefs

Education & Learning Support

We provide inclusive educational opportunities that empower children, youth, and adults to thrive academically and socially. We reach out to schools and learning spaces with teacher training, remedial learning programs, and classroom resources that strengthen quality teaching and promote retention. Our initiatives promote literacy and numeracy support, mentorship schemes, and digital literacy programs that equip learners with the skills needed for the modern world. We also foster lifelong learning through community learning centers, leadership clubs, and pathways to vocational training and higher education.

Learn More

Agriculture & Food Security

We promote sustainable agriculture and improved food security through climate-resilient farming practices and livelihood support. We provide training on improved agronomic techniques, organic and conservation agriculture, and post-harvest handling to boost productivity and reduce losses. Our initiatives reach out with community demonstration farms, seed distribution, agro-input access, and training in value-added processing. We promote market linkages, cooperative development, and nutrition education to increase incomes and ensure households have access to sufficient, safe, and nutritious food year-round.

Learn More

Peace and Conflict Resolution

We promote peaceful, cohesive communities by strengthening dialogue, mediation, and conflict prevention mechanisms. We provide inclusive peacebuilding initiatives such as community mediation, peace education, youth peace clubs, reconciliation events, and early-warning systems. Our initiatives empower communities to resolve disputes, reduce tensions, and build lasting social harmony.

Learn More

Community Development

We provide holistic community development initiatives that empower communities to identify priorities, take ownership of solutions, and lead sustainable change. We guide communities in developing Community Action Plans (CAPs)—strategic documents outlining goals, activities, and resource needs—which are shared with donors, government agencies, and development partners to attract support while maintaining community ownership. Through capacity-building, participatory governance, and integration of cross-sector priorities, we strengthen inclusive leadership, social cohesion, and long-term community resilience.

Why We Need You to Become a Volunteer

KiiD relies on the dedication and expertise of volunteers to extend our reach and impact in communities across Nigeria. By joining our team, you become part of a movement creating positive change.

Make a Meaningful Difference

Working directly with communities in need allows you to see the immediate impact of your contributions. Whether you're helping with health outreach, environmental initiatives, or skills training, your time and talents directly improve lives.

Develop New Skills

Volunteering with KiiD provides opportunities to gain valuable experience in community development, project management, and cross-cultural communication. These skills enhance both personal growth and professional development.

Join a Dedicated Community

Connect with like-minded individuals who share your passion for sustainable development and social justice. Our volunteers build lasting relationships while working toward common goals of community empowerment and environmental stewardship.
