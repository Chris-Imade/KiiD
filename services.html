Our Programs

  • Home
  • Our Programs

Health Advocacy

We reach underserved communities with mobile medical services, maternal and child health support, and disease prevention education.

Environmental Protection

We promote eco-literacy through climate clubs, tree planting campaigns, WASH programs and renewable energy workshops.

Socio-Economic Empowerment

Our initiatives include vocational training, entrepreneurship development, community development, economic inclusion, and support for women, youth, and vulnerable individuals.

Rehabilitation Programs

We provide mental health and psychosocial support, child protection, disability inclusion, family strengthening and protection services for GBV, displaced and vulnerable individuals.

Get Involved

Why We Need You to Become a Volunteer

KiiD relies on the dedication and expertise of volunteers to extend our reach and impact in communities across Nigeria. By joining our team, you become part of a movement creating positive change.

Make a Meaningful Difference

Working directly with communities in need allows you to see the immediate impact of your contributions. Whether you're helping with health outreach, environmental initiatives, or skills training, your time and talents directly improve lives.

Develop New Skills

Volunteering with KiiD provides opportunities to gain valuable experience in community development, project management, and cross-cultural communication. These skills enhance both personal growth and professional development.

Join a Dedicated Community

Connect with like-minded individuals who share your passion for sustainable development and social justice. Our volunteers build lasting relationships while working toward common goals of community empowerment and environmental stewardship.
