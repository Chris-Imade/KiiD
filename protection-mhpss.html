Our Program

Protection & Mental Health and Psychosocial Support (MHPSS)

We provide trauma-informed protection and mental health support for individuals facing distress, violence, exploitation, displacement, or reintegration challenges. Our services restore dignity, resilience, and emotional stability.

What We Do

Core Activities

Psychological First Aid & Counselling

Individual and group therapy sessions.

Community-Based MHPSS

Safe spaces, social support groups, and recreational therapy.

Case Management

Assessments, safety planning, and referrals for GBV, trafficking, and IDP survivors.

Child Protection Support

Parenting sessions, child-friendly spaces, and risk monitoring.

Anti-Trafficking Awareness

Community education, survivor support, and reintegration assistance.

Disability Inclusion Measures

Accessible support and assistive referrals.
