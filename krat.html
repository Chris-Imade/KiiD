About KRAT

The KIID Research and Assessment Team (KRAT) is the volunteer-driven research, field, and analytical arm of Kingscity Initiative for Integrated Development (KIID). KRAT is made up of highly trained volunteer members present across all 36 states and the FCT, enabling rapid, nationwide field coverage for research and assessment activities.

As the core engine behind KIID's evidence-generation work, KRAT conducts high-quality field research, surveys, assessments, and evaluations that guide programme design, strengthen monitoring, and enhance learning.

Every KRAT volunteer brings a blend of strong technical skills, ethical research standards, and a deep commitment to community development—making KRAT one of Nigeria's most trusted grassroots research networks.

KRAT ensures all interventions are:

  • Data-driven – informed by accurate, timely, and reliable evidence
  • People-centered – shaped by the lived realities of individuals and communities
  • Ethical & transparent – grounded in best practices for responsible research
  • Context-sensitive – reflecting genuine community needs and local dynamics
What We Do
  • Field-based surveys & assessments
  • Baseline, midline, and endline studies
  • Monitoring, Evaluation & Learning (MEL) support
  • Qualitative & quantitative research
  • Data cleaning, analysis, visualization & reporting
  • Community engagement & participatory research
  • Evidence generation for programmes, policies & advocacy
Why KRAT?
  • Nationwide presence across all 36 states & FCT
  • Trained volunteer enumerators with strong methodological skills
  • Rapid deployment capability for time-sensitive fieldwork
  • Strong ethical and quality assurance standards
  • Accurate, credible, and context-grounded data
  • Deep community entry and trust

Hire KRAT for External Projects

KRAT services are available for external contracting. We support NGOs, INGOs, Research Firms, Government Agencies, CSOs, Foundations, and Donors seeking credible, ethical, and timely field data across Nigeria.
